Berlin

  1. Museum island; which includes the Berlin Museum and the Old National Gallery.
  2. The Reichstag.
  3. Brandenburg Gate.
  4. Memorial to Sinti and Roma's.
  5. East Side Gallery to view the 1316 metres of the Berlin Wall still intact.
  6. DDR Museum, an interactive museum about the lives of East Berliners.
  7. Catch a river boat down the Spree.
  8. Postsdame Plaza and the Topography of Terror. The Topography of terror contains another piece of the Berlin Wall and a timeline of events, running from the Third Reich into Soviet Rule.
  9. Stasi Musuem. Which is brimming with information regarding the reign of Terror through Germany in the original Stasi Headquaters.
  10. Tunnels through Berlin which were used during the war and to escape during the Soviet Occupation. Tours are called 'Berlin from Below.'
