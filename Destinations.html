Rent a little party boat in Mallorca?

Em here - fancy a short trip sailing around Mallorca and Ibiza etc? Thought it might be fun to see these sites. May 2019!

  1. Formentor Beach. Located on the Island of Illa de Formentor with perfectly clear water.
  2. Porto Cristo. With great marinas and the underground cave system of lakes, Caves of Drach. Adults 14.50 euro for a guided tour with a classical quartet and boat ride on Lake Martel.
  3. Cala Santenyi. Southeast of Mallorca. The famous ‘es pontas’ stone arch is found here. The rock has been eroded by the water over years causing a large hole to be formed in the rock which has been labelled ‘the bridge’ by locals.
  4. Cala Boquer. Only accessible by boat, of foot, this secluded beach looks perfect for peaceful snorkelling.
  5. Resort of Sant Elm which has numerous restaurants overlooking the Natural Parl of Sa Dragonera.
  6. Palma Cathedral and fortress in Mallorca. (Historic site)
  7. Llotja Mallorca (Historic site)
  8. Iglesia de Sant Bartomeu.
